About

Hello, I’m Griever.
I’m interested in romhacking and fan translation of consoles games. My mother-tongue is Russian and I’m a proud member of translation group, named Magicteam. I’ve committed a few translations in Russian and wrote several tools. I code mainly in Haskell, Pascal, C, and assembler for target platforms, of course.
My education and main job are not related to computers at all, so I usually do some little projects in my spare time.
